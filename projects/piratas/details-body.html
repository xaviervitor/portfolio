Piratas is a top-down arcade shooter. You control a ship with "tank controls" and can make points by destroying other pirate ships, with the objective of surviving the most time possible. When the player makes enough points, a harder pirate ship, the Ghost Ship, appears. Destroying it has the benefit of recovering part of the player's health, creating a game-loop that encourages constant movement and active seeking of enemies.

Features

  • Enemy AI: Easy to inherit, with interfaces for overriding behaviors at states defined by a state machine;
  • Architecture: Most behaviors make heavy use of events, making code easier to read;
  • Visual polish: Water distortion shader, particles, using repurpused asset pack sprites and layering;
  • Sounds: Sound randomization and layering, making the game more immersive;
